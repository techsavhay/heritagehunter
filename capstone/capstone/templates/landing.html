Welcome to Heritage Hunter!


This Web App is designed to guide users in discovering and visiting the UK's most unspoilt, historic pubs. I have specifically featured those venues considered nationally important, or 'three-star', as determined by CAMRA's pub heritage group. Their invaluable work has been instrumental in the creation of this app, and I extend my utmost gratitude for their ongoing efforts. I strongly encourage all visitors to explore https://pubheritage.camra.org.uk/ to learn more about the pubs they plan to visit.
