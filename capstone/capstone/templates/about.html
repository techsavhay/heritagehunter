About Heritage Hunter

What is Heritage Hunter?

Heritage Hunter serves as a guide to some of the most architecturally renowned pubs in the United Kingdom. Whether you're a seasoned pub-goer or someone interested in the architectural details that give each pub its unique identity, Heritage Hunter is designed to help you discover and appreciate these gems.

Why Was Heritage Hunter Created?

It was born out of a passion for pubs, history, architecture and also as a good project to help me improve my computing coding skills.
